Welcome to **PokeChess**, where strategy meets the Pokemon world! This guide will help you understand the unique features, game modes, and progression system.

## üéÆ Game Modes

### 1. VS Computer (AI Rival)
Challenge a virtual Rival Trainer powered by advanced AI.
*   **The Rival**: Your opponent isn't just a chess bot; they have a personality. They will trash-talk, comment on your blunders, and react to big plays.
*   **Difficulty Levels**:
    *   **Easy**: Good for beginners. The AI plays casually.
    *   **Medium**: A standard club-level challenge.
    *   **Hard**: Powered by the advanced **Gemini 3.0 Pro** model. Expect no mercy.

### 2. Local PvP
Play against a friend on the same device. The board flips automatically after every move.

### 3. Online Multiplayer
Play against a friend remotely.
1.  Select **Online** from the main menu.
2.  **Host Game**: Generates a unique Room Code. Send this to your friend.
3.  **Join Game**: Paste the code provided by the host to connect.
*   *Note: This uses a direct connection. No account registration is needed.*

### 4. Trainer Tower
A fast-paced **Puzzle Rush** mode.
*   You have **60 seconds** on the clock.
*   Solve chess tactics (mates, forks, pins) to earn points.
*   **Correct Move**: +10 Points.
*   **Wrong Move**: Time penalty.
*   *Great for farming Coins and XP!*

## ‚öîÔ∏è Gameplay Features

### Elemental Combat
Capturing pieces isn't just a sound effect‚Äîit's a battle!
*   **Electric Types** (e.g., Pikachu): Cast **Thunderbolt**.
*   **Fire Types** (e.g., Charizard): Unleash **Fire Blast**.
*   **Water Types** (e.g., Blastoise): Use **Hydro Pump**.
*   **Psychic/Ghost**: Cast **Shadow Ball** or **Psychic**.
*   **Rock/Ground**: Crush enemies with **Rock Slide**.
*   **Critical Hits**: Sometimes, a capture will trigger a "CRITICAL HIT" animation, earning you bonus XP.

### Game Variants
You can change the rules in the "Battle Setup" menu:
*   **Standard**: Normal Chess rules. Checkmate the King.
*   **King of the Hill (KOTH)**: A faster, aggressive variant. You win by Checkmate **OR** by moving your King to one of the four center squares (`d4`, `e4`, `d5`, `e5`).

## üõçÔ∏è Economy & Progression

### Trainer Profile
*   **XP & Levels**: Earn XP for every move, capture, check, and win. Leveling up upgrades your Trainer Card status.
*   **Leagues**: Win ranked games against the AI or Online to increase your Rating. Climb from **Bronze** to **Master Ball** Tier.
*   **Missions**: Complete daily tasks (e.g., "Capture 5 Pokemon") to earn bonus rewards.

### The PokeMart
Spend your hard-earned Coins here.
*   **Army Themes**: Unlock new piece sets like the **Fire Army** or **Psychic Army**.
*   **Shiny Charm**: A special item that triples the chance of your pieces appearing as **Shiny Pokemon** (rare color variants).
*   **Cosmetics**: Buy Gold backgrounds for your profile or accessories like Ash's Hat.

## üêû Tips & Tricks
*   **Shiny Pokemon**: Every time a game starts, there is a 5% chance a piece will be Shiny (sparkling and different color). If you own the **Shiny Charm**, this chance jumps to 25%!
*   **Chat**: In AI mode, you can chat with the Rival. Try asking them why they made a move, or taunt them!
*   **Streaks**: Winning 3 games in a row sets your board "On Fire", giving it a special visual glow.
